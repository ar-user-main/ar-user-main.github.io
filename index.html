Adrien Ragot

Since october 2022, I am PhD student in Computer Science and Mathematics, doing a joint PhD--project between the Laboratoire d'informatique de Paris Nord (LIPN) of Université Paris 13 and the Dipartimento di Matematica e Fisica of Universitá degli studi Roma tre.
I am supervised by Stefano Guerrini, Thomas Seiller, and Lorenzo Tortora de Falco.
I am funded by the Université Franco-Italienne/ Universitá Italo-Francese.

Before that, from november 2020 to october 2021, I was a Doctoral Researcher in the SaToSS research group at the University of Luxembourg, where I was supervised by Sjouke Mauw and Matteo Acclavio.

I received a double master degree in october 2020 in Mathematics from Université Aix-Marseille and Universitá degli Studi Roma tre via the Binational Curriculum in Logic.
I received a Bachelor degree in June 2018 in Mathematics from Université de Tours.
