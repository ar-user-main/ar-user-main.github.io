Since october 2022, I am PhD student in Computer Science and Mathematics, doing a joint PhD--project between the LIPN of Université Paris 13 and the Departimento di Matematica e Fisica of Roma Tre. I am supervised by Stefano Guerrini, Thomas Seiller, and Lorenzo Tortora de Falco. I am Funded by the Université Franco-Italienne / Università Italo-Francese. Before that, from november 2020 to october 2021, I was a Doctoral Researcher in the SaToSS group at the University of Luxembourg, where I was supervised by Sjouke Mauw and Matteo Acclavio.
